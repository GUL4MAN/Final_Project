Silog

Silog is a popular Filipino breakfast meal. The name tosilog was derived from the different components of the meal which are:Sinangag (Filipino garlic fried rice), and Sunny-side up egg.



    Ingredients


  • 3 cups sinangag
  • 3 sunny-side up eggs
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil

    • Steps


  • Step 1. Heat the pan, and pour the cooking oil

  • Step 2. Cook the (sunnyside up)

  • Step 3. Meanwhile, remove the excess oil in the cooking pot where the egg was cooked. Heat the same pot and put-in the sinangag. Cook the fried rice for 1 minute while tossing.

  • Step 4. Serve Share and enjoy!
