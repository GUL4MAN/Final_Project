Tosilog

Tosilog is a popular Filipino breakfast meal. The name tosilog was derived from the different components of the meal which are: Tocino (cured pork), Sinangag (Filipino garlic fried rice), and Sunny-side up egg. Tosilog is best eaten with a spicy vinegar dip.



    Ingredients


  • 1 lb. uncooked tocino
  • 3 cups sinangag
  • 3 sunny-side up eggs
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil

    • Steps


  • Step 1. Boil the water in a cooking pot.

  • Step 2. Once the water starts to boil, pour-in the cooking oil and add the uncooked tocino. Boil in medium heat until the water evaporates.

  • Step 3. Once the water completely evaporate, the oil will be left behind. Let one side of the tocino cook in oil for 3 minutes. Flip the tocino and cook the other side for the same time.

  • Step 4. Arrange the cooked tocino with the eggs in individual plates.

  • Step 5. Meanwhile, remove the excess oil in the cooking pot where the tocino was cooked. Heat the same pot and put-in the sinangag. Cook the fried rice for 1 minute while tossing. The residue from the tocino will stick to the fried rice making it more tasty.

  • Step 6. Arrange the fried rice with the tocino in individual plates.

  • Step 7. Serve with spiced vinegar.

  • Step 8. Share and enjoy!
