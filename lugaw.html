Lugaw

Lugaw or plain congee is the most basic congee recipe that only requires rice, water, and salt. This version is somewhat upgraded because I used an ingredient that improves the flavor of the dish – Rousong. Rousong is made from pork. This is also called pork or meat floss.



    Ingredients


  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 4 to 5 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup rousong pork floss

    • Steps


  • Step 1. Pour water in a cooking pot. Bring to a boil.

  • Step 2. Put-in the rice. Continue cooking for 30 minutes or until the texture becomes thick, while stirring once in awhile.

  • Step 3. Add the salt, stir and then cook for 2 minutes more.

  • Step 4. Transfer to a serving bowl. Top with a tablespoon of rousing.

  • Step 5. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
